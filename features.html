Built for Pakistan's daily ride

Hybrid efficiency with real motorcycle character.

Evo Volt Hybrid combines cleaner mobility, streetfighter design, and safety hardware for everyday urban riding.

Key features

Performance, safety, and smarter energy.

125cc + 48V Assist

Combined hybrid output supports fast city starts and smoother overtakes while keeping fuel use in check.

Regenerative Braking

Energy recovery during braking and deceleration helps recharge the battery during stop-and-go traffic.

Dual-Channel ABS

Better braking control in urgent conditions, paired with front and rear disc hardware.

48V Li-ion Battery

A compact battery compartment keeps the center of gravity low and protects the hybrid electronics.

LED Lighting

LED headlamp, tail lamp, and indicators sharpen visibility and match the bike's modern stance.

Trellis-Style Frame

The red frame language gives the brand a recognizable performance signature.
